Venue

VENUE ADDRESS
The Alexion evening lecture will take place at The Julia Morgan Ballroom which is located at 465 California Street, San Francisco, CA 94104.

The Julia Morgan Ballroom is situated within walking distance (approximately 1.3 kilometres) of the Moscone Center, and approximately 1.2 kilometres from Hotel Nikko.

TRANSFERS
Shuttles will be available to take you to the meeting venue from the congress centre at 20:00, and from Hotel Nikko at 19:30. If you would like to reserve a place on either of these shuttles, please tick the appropriate box in the registration form which can be accessed via the ‘Register Now’ tab at the top of this page.

Registration for the shuttles is not mandatory and additional spaces will be available on the day if required.
